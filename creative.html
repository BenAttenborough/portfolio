Cambridge Guide

I developed this app as part of my first foray into designing for mobile devices. The original project was my final piece for the Adobe Generation app design course in 2014. It won the overall course prize, a free subscription to the Adobe Creative Suite for a year.

The idea behind the app was to create a mobile an interactive tourist guide to Cambridge in the UK, where I was then studying.

It has certain advantages over a paper guide:

  • Up-to-date information on tourist hotspots
  • Link to the Google Maps API, whch allows users to find their way to the point of interest
  • Always with you as you explore the city, providing information as you need it
  • In order to implement the app I crafted the site using HTML and CSS and added interaction and UI features with jQuery Mobile.
